Virtual Cursor

Cursor

Control a virtual cursor on screen using your hand — move, aim, and click without touching anything.

How cursor mode works
👍
Hold Thumb Up (~0.4 s)
Hold your thumb up to toggle cursor mode on or off
🖐
Open palm → move cursor
Move your wrist within the Cursor Zone to position the cursor on screen
✊
Close fist → click
Hold palm open for a moment, then close your fist to fire a click at the cursor position
All cursor mode gestures
🖐
Open palm → move cursor
Keep palm open and move your wrist within the Cursor Zone
✊
Fist → left click
Hold palm open ≥ 0.2 s, then close fist — click fires at the dot
☝
Pointing + swipe → scroll / action
Swipe with finger pointed — maps to your ☝ Pointing settings below (e.g. swipe down → Scroll down)
✌
Victory + swipe → navigate / action
Swipe with peace sign — maps to your ✌ Victory settings below (e.g. swipe left → Go back)
Recommended cursor-mode setup
💡
In Settings, configure ☝ Pointing and ✌ Victory swipes
Try: ☝ swipe up → Scroll up, ☝ swipe down → Scroll down, ✌ swipe left → Go back, ✌ swipe right → Go forward. These gestures fire in both normal and cursor mode.
